Slender Man

The Slender Man is a thin, unnaturally tall man with a featureless face who wears a black suit. According to legend, he can stretch or shorten his arms at will and has tentacle-like appendages protruding from his back.

Jeff the Killer

Jeff the Killer is a story about a boy who becomes a crazed murderer after being bullied and attacked by other kids. His disfigured face, wide smile, and homicidal tendencies make him a terrifying figure.
